About

What the club is about

R.U.G.V.E.D is a student-led defense technology initiative focused on creating Remote Unmanned Ground Vehicular Electronic Defence systems. The club brings together students across artificial intelligence, robotics, electronics, mechanical systems, management, and research to build practical solutions for real-world defense and autonomous mobility challenges.

The goal is to bridge the gap between innovation and application through military-grade automation, autonomous systems, intelligent navigation, embedded control, and scalable engineering. The team’s work reflects both competitive success and sustained technical development across multiple domains.

A detailed timeline of milestones

Featured in National Defence Expo

R.U.G.V.E.D gained recognition in the defense technology space through its presence at the National Defence Expo 2025, reflecting visibility beyond student competition circuits and into larger innovation platforms.

2024

Smart India Hackathon — Top 5

The team secured a Top 5 finish in Smart India Hackathon 2024, marking one of its strongest national-level achievements and highlighting both technical depth and execution capability.

2024

WALRUS 2.0 Successfully Deployed

WALRUS 2.0 was successfully deployed as a working prototype, showing progress from concept to implementation in autonomous and modular military vehicle design.

Patent Filed for AI-based Terrain Navigation

A patent filing for AI-based terrain navigation reflects the team’s research-oriented innovation in autonomous movement, terrain analysis, and intelligent path planning.

AI for Change — Online Hackathon Winners

Winning AI for Change 2024 demonstrates the team’s ability to apply artificial intelligence to meaningful engineering problems under competitive conditions.

The team earned a Top 7 finish at BITS Goa in a line-following robot event, reinforcing its strength in robotics performance, control logic, and system tuning.
